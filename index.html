Video Game Interfaces!

Sophia Bae + Ali Salifov

A curated collection exploring video game interfaces, focusing on HUDs, menus, maps, and diegetic UI. The blocks examine how games communicate information, engage player behavior, and blend usability with a game's visual identity.
